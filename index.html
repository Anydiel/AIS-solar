☀️ PANEL SPECIFICATIONS
VocVoltage Open Circuit — Max voltage, used for string sizing
VmpVoltage at Maximum Power — Operating voltage
ImpCurrent at Maximum Power — Operating current
IscShort Circuit Current — For fuse/breaker sizing

MPPTMaximum Power Point Tracking — Efficient solar charging

🔋 BATTERY & BMS
SoCState of Charge — Battery percentage
DoDDepth of Discharge — Usable capacity
LVDLow Voltage Disconnect — Protection cutoff
LVRLow Voltage Reconnect — Restart voltage
BMSBattery Management System — Protects lithium batteries
CAN Bus / RS485Communication protocols between battery and inverter
