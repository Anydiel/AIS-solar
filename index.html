🏠 STEP 1: Roof Railing Installation
🔧 HOW TO INSTALL: Locate rafters → Mark positions → Install L-feet with lag bolts → Attach aluminum rails → Use level → Seal all penetrations.
⚠️ For Philippines: Use stainless steel hardware. Add extra L-feet every 2ft for typhoon resistance.
☀️ STEP 2: MC4 Connectors & Panel Series Wiring
🔴 SERIES RULE: Panel1 NEG (-) → Panel2 POS (+). Panel2 NEG (-) → Panel3 POS (+). Panel3 NEG (-) → Panel4 POS (+). Free ends: Panel1 POS (+) and Panel4 NEG (-) to MPPT.
⚠️ NEVER exceed MPPT max input voltage! Check calculator specs above.
⚡ STEP 3: Solar Charge Controller (MPPT)
🔴 CONNECTION ORDER (CRITICAL): Connect Battery FIRST to MPPT → Then connect Solar Panels. Never reverse this order!
⚠️ Reverse polarity will destroy the MPPT controller! Double-check with a multimeter.
🔋 STEP 4: Battery Series Wiring (12V → 24V / 48V)
🔴 SERIES STEPS: BAT A NEG (-) → BAT B POS (+). System POS (+) → BAT A POS (+). System NEG (-) → BAT B NEG (-). For 48V: use 4 batteries in series.
⚠️ Use IDENTICAL batteries! Never mix old and new batteries in series.
🔌 STEP 5: Inverter Connection (Battery → Inverter)
🔴 CONNECTION: Battery POSITIVE (+) → T-Class Fuse (within 7 inches) → Inverter DC+. Battery NEGATIVE (-) → Direct to Inverter DC-.
⚠️ MOST IMPORTANT safety device! Use T-class fuse for lithium batteries.
⛓️ STEP 6: Earthing (Grounding) - SAFETY CRITICAL
⛓️ CRITICAL FOR SAFETY: Connect ALL metal parts (panels, rails, MPPT case, inverter case) to earth ground rod using 10 AWG copper wire.
⚠️ PROPER GROUNDING PROTECTS AGAINST ELECTRIC SHOCK AND EQUIPMENT DAMAGE! Never skip this step.
📡 COMPLETE SYSTEM: All Components Connected
✅ STARTUP SEQUENCE: Breaker 2 (Battery) ON → Breaker 1 (Solar) ON → T-Fuse ON → Inverter ON
