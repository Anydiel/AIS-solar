🏠 STEP 1: Roof Railing Installation
🔧 HOW TO INSTALL: Locate rafters → Install L-feet with lag bolts → Attach aluminum rails → Use level → Seal penetrations.
⚠️ Philippines: Use stainless steel hardware. Add extra L-feet every 2ft for typhoon resistance.
☀️ STEP 2: MC4 Connectors & Panel Series Wiring
🔴 SERIES RULE: Panel1 NEG (-) → Panel2 POS (+). Panel2 NEG (-) → Panel3 POS (+). Free ends to MPPT.
⚡ STEP 3: Solar Charge Controller (MPPT)
🔴 CRITICAL: Connect Battery FIRST to MPPT → Then connect Solar Panels. Never reverse!
⚠️ Reverse polarity destroys MPPT! Double-check with multimeter.
🔋 STEP 4: Battery Series Wiring (12V → 24V / 48V)
🔴 SERIES STEPS: BAT A NEG (-) → BAT B POS (+). System POS → BAT A POS. System NEG → BAT B NEG.
⚠️ Use IDENTICAL batteries! Never mix old and new.
🔌 STEP 5: Inverter Connection (Battery → Inverter)
🔴 CONNECTION: Battery POSITIVE (+) → T-Class Fuse (within 7 inches) → Inverter DC+.
⚠️ MOST IMPORTANT safety device! Use T-class fuse for lithium batteries.
⛓️ STEP 6: Earthing (Grounding) - SAFETY CRITICAL
⛓️ CRITICAL: Connect ALL metal parts (panels, rails, MPPT case, inverter case) to earth ground rod.
⚠️ PROPER GROUNDING PROTECTS AGAINST ELECTRIC SHOCK AND FIRE!
📡 COMPLETE SYSTEM: All Components Connected
✅ STARTUP SEQUENCE: Breaker 2 (Battery) ON → Breaker 1 (Solar) ON → T-Fuse ON → Inverter ON
